How it works

Step 1

You can get started by clicking on the sign up button in the top right corner of the screen and making an account!

Step 2

Once you are signed up, click the login button, enter your account details and you will be redirected to the user dashboard.

Step 3

On the user dashboard you will see some options. There will be a button called create which you can click to start making a survey.

Step 4

Fill out the form on the create a survey page and click submit at the bottom when you're done to create a survey!

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I make an account? How do I create a survey? How do I edit a survey? How do I delete a survey?

Click the sign up button in the top right corner and fill out the form to create an account.

If you haven't created an account, make one first. Then login by clicking the login button and entering your account details. Once you are logged it you will be redirected to the user dashboard where you will see all existing surveys and a button that says create. Click the create button and it will take you to a page where you can create a new survey.

From the user dashboard you will see a list of your existing surveys, you can click on one to see a detailed view of it. On the detailed view there will be an edit button near the top right corner of the page. Click on the edit button and it will take you to a page with a form and all survey content filled in. Make any changes that you want and click the save button at the bottom of the page to save your changes, which will be published automatically.

From the user dashboard you will see a list of existing surveys, click on one to see a detailed view of it. On the detailed view there will be a delete button near the top right corner of the page. Click on the delete button and you will see a confirmation message popup, click ok on the message and it will be deleted.
